What It Solves

Current problem

  • Social post ideas get lost before they are used.
  • Manual posting is inconsistent and depends on one person.
  • Approval is informal, which creates brand risk.
  • Results are hard to track across platforms.

Proposed system

  • One place for campaigns, drafts, approvals, and schedules.
  • Admin-controlled publishing workflow.
  • Reusable brand rules and content patterns.
  • Publishing logs and future analytics snapshots.

Workflow

1. Draft

Capture a post idea, platform, campaign, caption, and asset note.

2. Approve

Review tone, client safety, CTA, and brand fit before anything can publish.

3. Schedule

Move approved content into a timed queue managed by the dashboard.

4. Publish

Send approved posts through Buffer first, then add direct platform APIs later.
